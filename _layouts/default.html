A Message of Hope

May you be filled with Hope and encouragement in this time of uncertainty. God is in control and He has everything figured out. Stay connected in Him!

Saturday Night Services Cancelled

With the State of Iowa's request to not assemble with crowds of 10 or more, we will be canceling Saturday night services until further notice.

Please continue to pray for those who have been infected by this virus and for their families. Also, please pray for our Healthcare providers and government as they continue to put plans together to combat this virus.

Please be encouraged, Our God is bigger than all of this! We will be praying for you all!

The 5k will be virtual only

Please run wherever you want April 4th and then send us your times! We will still honor the awards but unfortunately we can't have the event at the Church and campground as planned.

We are so sorry for the inconvenience but your health and safety is the priority and we will try again next year.
